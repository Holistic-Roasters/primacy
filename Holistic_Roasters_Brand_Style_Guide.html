Brand Style Guide

A comprehensive guide to the Holistic Roasters brand identity

Brand Overview & Mission

Holistic Roasters' Biodynamic® Coffee is a premium, specialty-grade coffee brand committed to delivering the cleanest, healthiest coffee available. Sourced from family-run, regenerative farms certified by Demeter (the global standard for biodynamic agriculture), this coffee is lab-tested for purity and free from mold, mycotoxins, heavy metals, and harmful chemicals.

Mission Statement

We believe that regenerative farming is one of the most powerful forces for positive change on our planet. Every cup of coffee represents an opportunity to improve soil health, enhance biodiversity, and support farming communities that are leading this vital transformation.

Our commitment is simple but profound: to source and roast the world's finest Biodynamic® and regeneratively grown Organic coffees. This means cultivating equitable partnerships with farmers, maintaining unwavering standards in our roasting practice, and bringing out the full potential of every bean we source.

By choosing Holistic Roasters, you're not just getting a cleaner, healthier coffee - you're supporting farmers who are bringing regenerative wisdom back to agriculture and helping create a more resilient future.

Word Choice

Preferred Terms

  • "Biodynamic" (always capitalized as a proper noun tied to Demeter certification)
  • "Regenerative" (emphasizes proactive environmental impact)
  • "Clean" (simple, relatable term for purity)
  • "Specialty-grade" (highlights quality)
  • "Health-supportive" (connects product to wellness)

Avoid

  • "Natural" (too vague and overused)
  • "Eco-friendly" (less specific than "regenerative")
  • Negative framing (e.g., "no pesticides" becomes "grown without harmful chemicals")

Health-Focused Positioning

Holistic Roasters positions its products as health-supportive, emphasizing purity and quality while maintaining scientific credibility.

Health Benefits to Emphasize

  • Free from mold and mycotoxins that can trigger inflammation
  • No heavy metals or contaminants that accumulate in the body
  • Grown without synthetic pesticides or chemicals
  • Higher antioxidant content from healthy soil
  • Supports clean, sustained energy without the crash

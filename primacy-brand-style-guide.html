Mission

PRIMACY is not a supplement company; we are a human performance company. Our mission is to engineer the world's most intelligent, integrated, and effective systems for mastering the 24-hour performance-recovery cycle.

Philosophy

Peak daytime output is a direct result of optimized nightly recovery. Performance and recovery are not separate events; they are two sides of the same coin.

Logo Usage Guidelines

  • Maintain clear space equal to the height of the central "eye" of the star
  • Never stretch, distort, rotate, or change proportions
  • Always place on clean, minimalist backgrounds (primarily black or dark grey)
  • The 8-pointed star represents the 8 functional ingredients

Responsive Behavior

All components are designed to be fully responsive. The grid system adapts from 4 columns on desktop to single column on mobile. Typography scales using clamp() for optimal readability across devices.

Design Principle: Mobile-first approach with progressive enhancement for larger screens.

Iconography Style

Icons follow a minimalist, single-weight line-art style. They should be functional, not decorative, rendered in Light Grey or White for neutral states, and adopt accent colors (APEX Green or RESET Blue) for active states.

Iconography Library

Minimalist line-art icons designed for functional clarity. All icons use 2px stroke weight at 24x24px base size.

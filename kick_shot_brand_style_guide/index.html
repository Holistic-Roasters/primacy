BRAND OVERVIEW

CORE IDENTITY

Precision-engineered neurochemical energy for high-performers. Cognitive fuel for those who refuse to waste time on mediocrity.

MISSION

To obliterate brewing bottlenecks and artisanal pretension with a ruthlessly efficient, nicotine-powered cognitive enhancer. An unapologetically potent tool for mental dominance.

VISION

Become the stimulant of choice for high-achievers, hackers, rebels & renegades who value speed, science, and unapologetic edge.

BRAND PERSONALITY

The Rebellious Scientist – ruthlessly efficient, darkly witty, dismissive of tradition, clinically bold, sharp, urgent, irreverent.

BRAND VALUES

  • Efficiency: Speed and effectiveness above all else.
  • Defiance: Rejecting norms, tradition, and unnecessary complexity.
  • Intelligence: Grounded in science, focused on cognitive optimization.
  • Intensity: Unapologetically potent and direct.
  • Authenticity: Raw, unfiltered, owning the controversy.

PRIMARY FONT: TRENCH 100

Grunge-industrial sans-serif. Raw, impactful, slightly distressed. Used for headlines, taglines, and impact statements. ALL CAPS often preferred for maximum impact.

TYPOGRAPHY RULES

  • Use size, weight, and color to establish clear visual hierarchy
  • Headlines (Trench 100) should dominate
  • Sub-heads and body copy (Space Grotesk) should be clean and subordinate
  • Maintain high contrast between text and background
  • ALL CAPS for Trench 100 headlines for maximum impact

LOGO

The Kick Shot logo is a bold circular icon featuring a central lightning bolt (the "shot") emerging from or surrounded by glitch-like radiating lines (the "kick"). It symbolizes cognitive ignition, high-voltage energy, and a digital/neurological "on" switch.

LOGO USAGE RULES

  • Maintain clear space around the logo (equivalent to the height of the lightning bolt)
  • Do not stretch, distort, recolor (except in approved single-color applications), or alter the logo elements
  • Do not add extraneous effects (shadows, glows) beyond the inherent distressed style

APPLICATIONS

The Kick Shot brand comes to life across various touchpoints, each maintaining the core identity while adapting to the specific medium.

PACKAGING

Single-serve stick sachets housed in a flip-top box resembling a high-design, minimalist cigarette pack. Predominantly Blackout Black with the logo prominent. Minimal text on the front. Neuro Yellow and Blood Red for key callouts and warnings.

SOCIAL MEDIA

High-contrast imagery, glitch effects (sparingly), bold typography. Sharp, witty, defiant tone. Use brand taglines and slogans as hashtags (#KickShot #NeuroRebellion #TLDB #StimulantsNotRituals #AntiRitual). Engage with users who embody the high-achiever/rebel persona.

WEBSITE

Dark theme (Blackout Black, Asphalt Gray). High contrast with Neuro Yellow and Toxic Cyan accents. Minimalist layout focusing on speed and clarity. Use Trench 100 for headlines, Space Grotesk for body. Dynamic elements like subtle glitch effects or animated lightning bolts.

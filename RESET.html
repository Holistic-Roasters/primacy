SYSTEM DIAGNOSTICS.

Will RESET make me groggy the next day?

No. This is by design. RESET is specifically formulated to avoid next-day grogginess. We intentionally rejected ingredients like high-dose melatonin and valerian root, which are known to cause a "sleep hangover." Our protocol is engineered for a clean reboot, not a fuzzy one.

Why no Melatonin?

We don't believe in using a brute-force hormone when intelligent signals work better. High-dose melatonin can disrupt your body's own natural production and lead to dependency. RESET is designed to provide the precursors and cofactors (like Magnesium and Apigenin) that help your brain produce its *own* melatonin at the correct time, leading to more natural, restorative sleep.

Is it safe to take RESET every night?

Yes. RESET contains no sedatives or dependency-forming ingredients. It is a formula of adaptogens, minerals, and amino acids designed to support your body's natural recovery processes. It is safe for consistent, nightly use as part of your performance protocol.

What does RESET taste like?

We call the flavor profile "Midnight Cherry." It is built on a base of functional Tart Cherry extract, then layered with notes of black cherry and vanilla for a rich, sophisticated flavor. It is balanced with zero-sugar monk fruit, making it a satisfying, non-sweet ritual to end your day.

Key Take-Aways

  1. Morning: APEX delivers rapid but controlled stimulation—caffeine/theanine peak, ketone dual-fuel, stable glucose—boosting executive function while preventing energy debt.
  2. Afternoon: Stimulant levels wane smoothly; theobromine and ketones maintain clarity, avoiding the classic mid-afternoon crash.
  3. Evening–Night: RESET initiates a multi-pathway downshift—cortisol suppression, GABAergic calm, anti-inflammatory action—reducing sleep latency and deepening slow-wave sleep.

This closed-loop architecture—APEX priming the day without burnout and RESET restoring the night—creates a virtuous 24-hour performance cycle that compounds benefits with consistent use.

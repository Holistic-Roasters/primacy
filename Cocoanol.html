THE EVIDENCE BASE

Cardiovascular System Optimization

The cardiovascular benefits of cocoa flavanols represent one of the most robust evidence bases in nutritional science. The FDA's qualified health claim acknowledges that high-flavanol cocoa powder may reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease.

The COSMOS trial, the largest randomized controlled trial on cocoa flavanols to date, demonstrated a 27% reduction in cardiovascular death among participants taking 500mg of cocoa flavanols daily. Our protocols deliver 200mg (APEX) and 150mg (RESET) of standardized flavanols through Cocoanol®.

Cognitive Enhancement & Neuroprotection

Research has demonstrated that cocoa flavanols are linked with improved thinking skills and cognitive performance. Studies show enhanced blood flow to the brain, improvements in memory and brain function, with effects particularly notable in older adults.

Molecular Mechanisms of Action

Cocoanol® delivers a standardized 20% flavanol content, ensuring consistent bioactive compound delivery. The primary mechanisms include:

1. Nitric Oxide Modulation

Cocoa flavanols, particularly epicatechin, enhance endothelial nitric oxide synthase (eNOS) activity, increasing NO bioavailability. This mechanism underlies the cardiovascular benefits.

2. Antioxidant Activity

Direct scavenging of reactive oxygen species and upregulation of endogenous antioxidant defenses, including catalase and superoxide dismutase.

3. Anti-Inflammatory Effects

Modulation of inflammatory pathways, including reduction of pro-inflammatory cytokines and adhesion molecules.

4. Mitochondrial Function

Enhancement of mitochondrial biogenesis and function, supporting cellular energy production and recovery.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

What makes Cocoanol® different from regular cocoa?

Cocoanol® is a proprietary cocoa extract standardized to contain 20% flavanols, making it 10-40x more concentrated than standard cocoa powder. The gentle extraction process preserves the delicate epicatechin compounds that are typically destroyed during conventional chocolate processing through fermentation and high-heat roasting.

Why different dosages in APEX vs RESET?

The dosing strategy is calibrated to match circadian physiology. APEX delivers 1000mg (200mg flavanols) to support peak daytime cardiovascular and cognitive demands. RESET provides 750mg (150mg flavanols) to facilitate overnight recovery without overstimulating systems during the rest phase. This differential dosing optimizes the 24-hour performance-recovery cycle.

What is the bioavailability timeline?

Cocoa flavanols demonstrate rapid absorption with detectable plasma levels within 30 minutes. Peak concentration (Cmax) occurs at 2-3 hours post-ingestion. The elimination half-life of epicatechin is approximately 2-3 hours, but metabolites persist longer. Chronic supplementation leads to steady-state accumulation of beneficial metabolites.

Are there any contraindications?

Cocoanol® contains minimal theobromine and caffeine compared to chocolate. However, individuals with severe caffeine sensitivity should note that trace amounts are present. The cardiovascular effects may potentiate certain medications. As with any protocol modification, consult your healthcare provider if you have pre-existing conditions or take medications affecting blood pressure or coagulation.

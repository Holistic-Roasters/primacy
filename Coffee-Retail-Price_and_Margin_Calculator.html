How to Use This Calculator

This tool helps determine suggested retail prices for roasted coffee type (Minivan, Rubicon, Bon Décaf, etc.) based on costs and target profit margins.

Global Inputs: Enter costs like Green coffee cost – based on the recipe (include cost of transportation and financing), Roast Shrinkage, Roast costs (include cost labour and equipment rental/usage), Packing, and Sticker cost. Set your desired Target Gross Margin (profit before overheads).

Currency & FX: Select your selling currency. Use the exchange rate if your green coffee cost is in a different currency (e.g., USD cost, CAD selling). Changing currency converts monetary inputs.

Tables Explained:

  • Suggested Retail Pricing: Shows unit cost (COGS) and the retail price needed to hit your target Gross Margin (GM). Adjust bag/container costs per size.
  • Net DTC Margins: Calculates Direct-to-Consumer Net Margin (NM) after accounting for Transportation (default 20%) and Overhead percentages (applied to the retail price).
    *** NOTE: Transactions fees of ~3% not included ***
  • Net B2B Margins: Calculates Business-to-Business Net Margin after applying a Discount to the retail price, plus Transportation (default 10%) and Overhead percentages.

All calculations update automatically as you change input values. Use the results as a guide for your pricing strategy.
